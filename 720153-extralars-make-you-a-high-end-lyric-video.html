About:

I will make you a professional lyric video suited your needs. I´ve worked with major labels like Universal Music and Sony Music and I have filmed artists like Tom Jones, Elton John and John Fogerty. I will deliver an artistic video with a major label quality for your project.

I will send you a video fitting for Youtube´s video conversion and one for television.
